3D Monster Model

This is a 3D Model that I made using Maya for my Independant Video Game project, Blind Fear. The inspiration for the design of this monster was from the common affliction of rams and goats both in the wild and captivity. Sometimes their horns can grow so long that they circle in on themselves and if not treated will eventually pierce their own skull. I took this idea and amplified it to have the horns pierce it's own eyes, making it fully blind as opposed to the player's own partial blindness.
