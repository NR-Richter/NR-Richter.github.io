Welcome!

This website is one of my many projects from my Junior year in the GIMM program at BSU. Though now defunct, when everything was set up properly, this website connects to ChatGPT and allows you to generate a team of 6 Pokemon for any game in the series (up to Legends ZA). It comes included with reccomended abilities, movesets, held items, and where each Pokemon can be obtained in the selected game.
