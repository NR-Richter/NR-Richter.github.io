Blind Fear

Blind Fear is a First Person Horror Game that I developed for my Independant Game Project. The game was developed in Unity over the course of my Junior Year. The goal of the game is to navigate and escape various labrynths while juggling multiple different obstacles, such as only being able to move when blind, avoiding dangerous monsters, and solving puzzles. Below is a video of the game when that was taken at the end of the spring semester of my Junior Year, so the game is not fully polished, but it is still a good representation of what I am capable of on my own.
